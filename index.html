Studying (researching) the Bible is another practical way to abide in the Word. The difference between reading and studying the Bible involves two additional activities: asking questions of the text and writing down your insight. You haven't really studied the Bible unless you've written your thoughts down on paper or comupter. --Rick Warren (The Purpose Driven Life, day24, p187)
The best way to become a 'doer of the Word' is to always write out an action step as a result of your reading or studying or reflecting on God's Word. Develop the habit of writing down exactly what you intend to do. --Rick Warren (The Purpose Driven Life, day24, p190)
The Bible was not given to increase our knowledge but to change our lives. -- D.L. Moody
"Your word is a lamp to my feet and a light for my path." (Psm119:105 NIV)
